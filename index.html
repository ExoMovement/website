Motivation

There are currently 5.4 million people in America suffering from paralysis. They cannot stand, walk or lift anything without some form of assistance and their medical bills can run upwards of $2.5 million over the course of their lives. This is unacceptable and from a first hand account, these people are unhappy with the current options available to them. For this reason we have decided to seek a viable solution to a literal multimillion dollar and people problem. We are working to create an affordable device to better the lives of these individuals and let them return to some form of normalcy.
